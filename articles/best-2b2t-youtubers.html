Best 2B2T YouTubers & Content Creators to Watch in 2026

From FitMC's legendary 2b2t history documentaries to SalC1's mind-blowing survival builds, this guide ranks the best 2B2T content creators by category so you can find the perfect channel whether you are a new player or a veteran looking for fresh perspectives.

Published: June 29, 2026 Last Updated: June 2026 Audience: 2b2t enthusiasts and new players Focus: 2b2t youtubers, 2b2t content creators, best 2b2t videos

2B2T has one of the richest content creator communities in all of Minecraft. For over a decade, YouTubers have documented the server's history, built insane bases, hacked their way through the wasteland, and produced some of the most iconic Minecraft videos ever made.

If you are new to 2B2T, YouTube is the best place to start. You will learn the culture, the history, and the practical skills before you even set foot on the server. This guide breaks down the top creators by category—so you can go straight to what interests you.

The Historian: FitMC

Channel: FitMC on YouTube

FitMC is the most famous 2B2T content creator by a wide margin. His documentary-style videos have millions of views each and have essentially defined how the internet understands 2B2T. His series "The Oldest Anarchy Server in Minecraft" covers the server's history from its founding in 2010 to the present day.

Best for: Players who want to understand 2B2T lore, famous bases, historical events like the Spawn Masons era, the Rusher War, and the evolution of the queue system.

Essential watch: "The Oldest Anarchy Server in Minecraft" series, "The Story of 2B2T's Most Famous Base"

Why FitMC matters: He is the gateway creator. Most 2B2T players discovered the server through his videos. Understanding his content means understanding how the community talks about itself.

The Builder & Adventurer: SalC1

Channel: SalC1 on YouTube

SalC1 produces high-production-value 2B2T survival content focusing on ambitious building projects, long-distance exploration, and creative problem-solving in the anarchy environment. His videos often showcase massive builds that took months to complete, contrasted against the constant threat of griefing.

Best for: Players who enjoy survival gameplay, base-building inspiration, and seeing what is possible when you dedicate months to a project.

Essential watch: "I Spent 100 Days on 2B2T," his highway exploration series

The Survivalist: Barney

Channel: Barney on YouTube

Barney is known for detailed, practical 2B2T survival guides that actually work. His videos cover everything from how to survive your first hour on the server to advanced stash-building techniques. Unlike the documentary-style creators, Barney focuses on teachable, repeatable strategies.

Best for: New players who need hands-on tips—how to get food, where to build a first base, how to avoid spawn killers, and what gear to prioritize.

Essential watch: "How to Survive Your First Day on 2B2T," "The Ultimate 2B2T Starter Kit"

The Entertainer: DoniBobes

Channel: DoniBobes on YouTube

DoniBobes brings humor and chaos to 2B2T content. His videos feature trolling, griefing, player interactions, and just the general absurdity of anarchy server life. If you want to laugh at the ridiculous things that happen when there are no rules, watch DoniBobes.

Best for: Entertainment, laughs, and insight into the social dynamics of the server (betrayal, alliances, and the occasional act of kindness in anarchy).

Essential watch: "I Trolled 2B2T With [Something Crazy]," his player interview series

The Veteran: Jake The Great

Channel: Jake The Great on YouTube

Jake The Great is one of the oldest active 2B2T content creators, having been on the server since circa 2016. His videos offer a longer-term perspective on how the server has changed, including coverage of major updates, queue system changes, and the evolving PvP meta.

Best for: Historical perspective and understanding how the server has evolved over time.

Essential watch: "2B2T Then vs Now," update reaction videos

Newcomers Making Waves in 2026

The 2B2T content scene is not static. New creators emerge regularly, especially after major updates. Here are three creators who have gained significant traction in 2026:

  • CortexPlays: Known for in-depth analysis of the 1.21.4 update's impact on 2b2t gameplay mechanics
  • AshTrader: Economy-focused content covering the 2b2t trading meta, player shops, and donor item market
  • Voidrunner: Speedrunner-style content documenting the fastest ways to achieve specific goals on 2b2t

Where Should You Start?

Not sure which creator fits you? Use this quick decision tree:

  • You want to understand the server's legend before you play: Start with FitMC's documentary series. You will learn why 2B2T matters.
  • You want to survive your first day: Watch Barney's survival guides, then read our Survival Guide for the written version.
  • You love epic builds: Subscribe to SalC1 and prepare to be amazed by what people build in a lawless world.
  • You just want to laugh: DoniBobes delivers the chaos. Watch after a bad queue day.

FAQ

Who is the most popular 2B2T YouTuber?

FitMC, by a wide margin. His most-viewed video has over 15 million views.

Are there any active 2B2T streamers?

Yes. Many creators stream on Twitch. Check FitMC (occasional streams), jakepz (regular exploration streams), and CortexPlays (weekly 2b2t analysis streams). Twitch discoverability for 2b2t content is lower than YouTube, but the community is active.

Do any creators still make old-style guide videos?

Barney and Megabyte produce the most practical tutorial content. For up-to-date guides on current mechanics, they are your best bet.

How do creators avoid getting griefed while filming?

Most film on alternate accounts or in remote base locations. FitMC famously films from a secure, undisclosed location. Some use private servers for cinematic shots (and disclose this).

Quick Tips

Start with FitMC for history, then Barney for survival skills, then SalC1 for inspiration.
