2B2T 1.21.4 Update Complete Guide: New Features, PvP Changes & Survival Strategies (2026)

2B2T has officially updated to Minecraft 1.21.4. This guide covers every meaningful change—the Mace in anarchy PvP, Trial Chambers on the server, Phantom behavior changes, known bugs, and how you should adapt your survival strategy.

Published: June 29, 2026 Last Updated: June 2026 Audience: 2b2t players of all levels Focus: 2b2t 1.21.4, 2b2t mace, 2b2t trial chambers

On June 23, 2026, 2B2T officially rolled out the Minecraft 1.21.4 update. For a server that ran on 1.12 for years, this is a seismic shift. New blocks, new combat mechanics, new structures, and new bugs. This guide breaks down everything that changed, what it means for your gameplay, and how to adapt.

The Mace in 2B2T PvP: Game-Changer or Gimmick?

The Mace is a new weapon added in Minecraft 1.21 that deals bonus damage based on fall distance. The formula: base 6 damage + (fall blocks fallen × 0.5) damage. Fall from 30 blocks, and you hit for 21 damage—over 10 hearts. Enough to one-shot a player in full Protection IV netherite armor.

How the Mace Works

  • Critical hits: Unlike swords and axes, Mace crits happen when you are falling AND hit an entity. The damage multiplier comes from fall distance, not jumping.
  • Wind Charge synergy: Using a Wind Charge to launch yourself upward, then falling onto an enemy with the Mace, is the optimal combo. Burst damage exceeds crystal PvP.
  • Density Enchantment: Increases damage per fallen block. At Density V, each block fallen adds +2.5 damage instead of +0.5. A 20-block density V mace hit deals ~56 damage (28 hearts).
  • Breach Enchantment: Reduces enemy armor effectiveness by up to 50%. Makes Mace effective even against fully-enchanted diamond/netherite opponents.

Does the Mace replace crystal PvP? No. But it adds a powerful third option to the combat triangle:

Getting a Mace on 2B2T

Maces are obtained from Ominous Vaults inside Trial Chambers. They can also be traded, but expect prices to be high initially. If you find a Trial Chamber, prioritize clearing the Ominous Vault (requires Ominous Trial Key from a captain-kill). The Mace cannot be repaired on an anvil, so each one has limited durability (250 uses, 500 with Unbreaking III). Stockpile several.

Warning: The Mace damage formula works on 2B2T. Players have already tested it. Ambushes from high ground are now lethal. Do not stand under open sky near known highway intersections without checking above you.

Trial Chambers on 2B2T: Worth the Risk?

Trial Chambers generate in the deepslate layer (Y=-40 to Y=-20) of newly generated chunks. On 2B2T, most explored terrain already exists. New chunks may generate them, but you will need to travel far from spawn in ungenerated directions.

What You Can Get

  • Ominous Vault loot: Mace (primary goal), Heavy Core (for Mace crafting), Ominous Bottles, enchanted books (Density, Breach, Wind Burst)
  • Regular Vault loot: Enchanted iron/diamond gear, golden apples, XP, arrows, name tags
  • Breeze Rods: Used to craft Wind Charges (needed for Mace combo) and the Mace itself
  • Ominous Trial Spawner XP: Excellent for fast XP farming once cleared

The Danger

Trial Chambers are enclosed underground spaces. If another player finds you inside, escape routes are limited. Wind Charges from mob drops will push you around unpredictably. Bring a hacked client with ESP and tracers to spot players before they spot you. The hacked clients guide has recommendations for underground ESP settings.

Do not go alone unless you are well-geared. Coordinate with a group or accept that you may lose your kit. The Trial Chambers are the first major dungeon-type structure in 2B2T’s history, and they will attract both treasure hunters and ambushers.

Phantom Changes and What They Mean

The official update notes that Phantoms now "function the same as vanilla Minecraft." Previously, 2B2T had custom phantom behavior that reduced their spawn rate and damage. With vanilla rules, phantoms spawn after 3+ in-game days without sleep, dealing 2 hearts of damage per hit on Hard difficulty.

Practical impact: Almost zero for experienced players. Most 2b2t players sleep in beds (in bases or portable beds) or use /kill to reset. Phantoms are a minor nuisance that can damage unenchanted leather/iron armor, but anyone with Protection IV diamond and above ignores them. New players leaving spawn should be aware—if you do not sleep, phantoms will find you.

Known Bugs and Issues (1.21.4)

As tracked on the official issues page, several bugs are being investigated. These are common for a major version jump on a heavily modified server:

  • Elytra flight inconsistencies: Some players report elytra gliding behavior differs from prior versions. Firework boosting may not work at expected speeds.
  • Portal linking issues: Nether portals in certain regions fail to link correctly. If your portal sends you somewhere unexpected, break and relight it.
  • Client compatibility: Older hacked clients (1.12-based) are obsolete. Update to clients that support 1.21.4. Check our hacked clients guide for version-compatible options.
  • Chunk loading edge cases: Border regions where 1.12 chunks meet new 1.21.4 chunks may have visual glitches or block collision issues.

Report bugs to bugs@2b2t.org. The server admin (Hausemaster / "K") is actively monitoring and fixing issues.

Updated Survival Strategy for 1.21.4

With new mechanics come new risks and opportunities. Here is how your 2B2T survival strategy should change:

Do Now

  • Update your client: Impact, Future, Wurst, and Meteor have 1.21.4 builds. Do not play on outdated clients.
  • Acquire a Mace: Find a Trial Chamber or trade for one. Carry it in your hotbar for high-ground ambushes.
  • Stock Wind Charges: Even if you do not use a Mace, Wind Charges are excellent mobility tools. Launch yourself over walls, across gaps, or upward to escape.
  • Check above you: The Mace meta means players will build height and drop on you. Do not AFK in open areas.

Do Later

  • Explore new terrain: Travel 50k+ blocks from spawn in a direction known to be unexplored to find Trial Chambers.
  • Farm Ominous Vaults: Once you find a Trial Chamber, clear it for Mace and enchanted book loot.
  • Update your base: Add ceiling protection against Mace drops. A simple overhang or roof prevents height-advantage attacks.

FAQ: 2B2T 1.21.4

Do I need to install 1.21.4 to play?

Yes. The server now runs 1.21.4. Connect with a 1.21.4 client. ViaVersion may work but is not guaranteed.

Will my old base be affected?

No. Existing terrain and builds are preserved. Only new chunk generation uses 1.21.4 features.

Is the Mace better than a Sharpness V Netherite Axe?

Situationally better. The Mace one-shots from height. The axe is more reliable at close range. Carry both.

Can I dupe Maces?
